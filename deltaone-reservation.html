About Delta Airlines


Delta Air Lines is a global airline that has been serving customers around the world for nearly a century. With a focus on providing high-quality service and a wide range of amenities, Delta is a popular choice for travelers of all types.

Delta’s fleet consists of more than 1,000 aircraft, including both narrow-body and wide-body planes. This allows the airline to offer a range of flight options to suit different needs, from short hops to long-haul international flights. The airline’s extensive route network includes more than 300 destinations in over 50 countries around the world, making it easy for travelers to find a flight to suit their needs.
