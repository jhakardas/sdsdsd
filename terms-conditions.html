Purchase & Confirmation of Travel Products

When you make a booking request for a Flight "Travel Product", Flightofox will send a "Booking Request" via airlines reservations systems for your flights to the airlines. "Flightofox" then processes your request and sends you a confirmation email when the booking is confirmed (e.g., when an itinerary has been ticketed). The "Booking Confirmation Email" is not a confirmation of tickets though. Note that the fares and availability of your booking request may change during the time we process your booking request. Your reservation is not confirmed and your purchase is not completed until "Flightofox" sends you an electronic confirmation "E-ticket".

In some cases "Flightofox" may determine that it is unable to confirm your flight booking tickets. Reason being:

  • Changes in Fare
  • Availability of flight
  • Airline's failure to confirm the ticket booking request
  • Incomplete or inaccurate payment information provided by customer
  • Rejection of a method or mode of payment, concerns that the ticket booking request was unauthorized or fraudulent
  • Other issues that may prohibit fulfillment of your flight ticket booking request.

In any case, Flightofox will notify you. If "Flightofox" is unable to process your ticket booking request, we may suggest you an alternative Travel Product. You are, however, under no any obligation to purchase any such alternative.

Useful Information for Travelers

Baggage Rules & Fees

Additional baggage fees may apply. For more information, click here: Baggage and Carry-On Fees. Since the baggage fees may change, we recommend that you contact with the airline you are traveling on for the latest information regarding airline specific baggage rules, requirements and fees.

Seats

If you have requested a seat to be assigned, we will send your request to book a seat to the airline. Requested Airline may or may not be able to confirm your request. At the time airlines may not be able to assign specific seats or seats sitting together. Occasionally reservation seats can be assigned only at the check-in counter at the airport.

Cancellation of Flights

Most airline reservation tickets are 100% non-refundable and non-transferable. In certain cases where the airline may allow cancellations, we will be only able to process refunds less Airline penalties. Please call or email us to find out the penalties. Flight Ticket cancellations/refunds must be processed prior to the scheduled departure time of the first flight segment by calling our customer service center. Incase of NO-SHOW tickets is 100% non-refundable.

Voluntary Flight Changes

Although the most itineraries / tickets allow changes, as per airline policy - the majority of these airline itinerary changes require the issuance of a new ticket. All the changes are subject to availability, airline rules and regulations, penalties and a difference from the original airfare and our service fees. The airline has the final authority regarding the reservation itinerary changes and penalties.

Involuntary Changes (Changes done directly by the airline)

Airlines may make changes to a scheduled flight itinerary that is beyond of our control. In these type of cases, if an airline system notifies us with a viable option for you, we will notify you with the changes as well as the option the airline is offering. If the airline does not provide us any viable option, as a valued customer, your flight ticket reservation will be reviewed and assisted by one of our Schedule Change Specialists.

Refund timelines

Flightofox may take up to 3 weeks or more to process your refund wit your payment method depending upon airline terms and conditions for refunds. Once your refund is processed, your financial institution may take up to 10 business days to post the credit to your account, and in some cases it takes upto 2 billing cycles on your statement to show the credit information.

Your refund will be processed to your original method(s) / mode of payment

Your Flightofox refund receipt email contains details about your refund amount, how, and when you'll get your refund.

If your credit / debit card statement includes a charge from a third party (e.g., a low-cost airline, other suppliers), you will receive your refund from that third party, not Flightofox. Please contact the respective third party regarding refund timelines and related questions.

Valid Photo ID

A valid government approved photo I.D. must be presented by all travelers in order to board a flight. Minors traveling domestically with parents generally do not need any photo I.D. If traveling with children less than two (2) years old, a birth certificate may be needed to confirm the infant's age. More information

Domestic Flights (within United States) Checking in

  • It is recommended that you should check-in at least three (3) hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time for International flights.
  • Please allow extra time if traveling with the children or need assistance for boarding the aircraft

Checking in for International Flights

  • It is recommended that you check-in at least three (3) hours prior to the scheduled flight departure time
  • Please allow extra time if traveling with the children or need assistance for boarding the aircraft
  • NOTE: The airlines reserves the right to deny the boarding to passengers that do not arrive at the Ticket Counter or Departure Gate with adequate time to check-in. Check-in times can be vary for certain cities and airlines, so it’s recommended that you confirm the timing with the airline you are flying.

Passport / Visas

For international travel, all passengers must be in possession of valid travel documents such as tickets, passport, visas, transit visas, Schengen Visas and all other entry permits to board a flight. Your passport must be valid for 6 months from your return date. It is solely the responsibility of the passenger(s) to arrange for all the documents needed to enter the country you are traveling to, or passing through the in transit. Please note, a roundtrip or ongoing ticket may be required for certain international cities. If you are traveling one-way, please verify with the airline or Consulate General to prevent any issues at time of flight boarding.

Operated by... (Flight number/details)

Please note that your flight booking itinerary may consist of flights that are operated by what is called a “CodeShare” partner of the airline on which you are traveling. It is for this reason we advise you to make sure that you check-in and board to your flight at the corresponding airline’s terminal without any failure. For example – The flight number on your ticket booking itinerary may indicate a United Airlines flight number but in fact this flight may be “Operated by Air Canada" in which case you are required to check-in at the Air Canada ticket counter and the boarding process will be the corresponding Air Canada gate. The same would be likewise for other airlines too.
